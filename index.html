"Friends Quotes Game"







How to play

This game is truly played by house rules. There are no set rules to follow and you can play it however you like. However for those who need some guidance, simply select what type of quotes you want. It can range from specific characters in a selected episode or even the entire transcript from the show. Once you've selected what type of quotes you want, hit the submit button and it should take you to a page filled with the selected quotes. Read the selected quotes aloud, or simply pick a smaller portion of those quotes to read aloud, and ask the person you're playing with to guess either who said it, or if you really want a challenge, what episode and season the quote was said in. Each correct guess gets one point and the first to 10 points wins!
